SlamDunker

This is a provisional, proof of concept demonstration on how we can get a first estimate on tweets that may originate from a person with malicious intentions. This model is quite eager in tagging a tweet as potential troll. Play around with it by typing your own tweet, or grabbing troll or nontroll tweets from our dataset!

We are in no way afiliated with Twitter and this project is only meant to be used for the course "Intelligent Systems Project" at UPC Barcelona.
